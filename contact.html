Common Questions

FAQ

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes! We deliver across Kenya. Delivery times vary by location — Nairobi orders are typically next-day, upcountry 2–4 business days.

Are your fireworks safety certified?

Absolutely. All our products meet KEBS standards and are imported from certified manufacturers. We include safety instructions with every order.

Do you do custom event displays?

Yes! Our team can design and execute a custom fireworks display for your event. Contact us at least 2 weeks in advance for large events.

What's your minimum order for wholesale?

Wholesale orders start at KES 50,000. We offer competitive pricing for retailers and event companies. Reach out via the contact form above.

Can I pick up my order?

Yes, collection is available from our Westlands warehouse Monday–Saturday, 8am–5pm. Please schedule in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash on delivery for Nairobi orders. Payment details are confirmed upon order approval.
